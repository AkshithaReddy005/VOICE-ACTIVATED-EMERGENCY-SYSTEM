Welcome to Alertify

Why Alertify?

Alertify is our innovative project designed to enhance personal safety by providing voice-activated alerts and location tracking in emergencies.

Comprehensive Protection

With Alertify, you can trigger emergency response by saying the keyword "Alertify." This ensures that you remain connected and protected in critical situations.

User Friendly

Our walkthrough menu and videos guide you through setting up Alertify with ease. Find additional information on our FAQ page.

Latest Technology

Alertify utilizes advanced voice activation technology that automatically triggers emergency alerts when the keyword "Alertify" is recognized. SMS notifications are sent automatically, with a manual activation option available as a backup.

Recording

Alertify records up to 15 minutes of actual events during emergencies, stored locally and on your personal cloud for future retrieval.

GPS with MPH Data

Your emergency contacts and first responders will see your exact location when you trigger an alert, along with speed data if applicable.
